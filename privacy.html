1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Dietician AI (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the “App”). By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

A) Personal & Health Information (you provide)

  • Age – to generate accurate diet plans and calculations.
  • Gender – to personalize nutrition recommendations.
  • Height – to calculate body metrics for diet planning.
  • Weight – to generate diet charts and track progress.
  • Location (user-provided) – to tailor plans to regional foods and availability.
  • Allergies – to exclude restricted items from your plan.
  • Diet Type (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, keto) – to customize recommendations.
  • Diet Data (diet plans/charts you create or save) – stored so you can access saved plans, including after reinstalling.

B) Device & Technical Information (collected automatically)

  • Device Model, Operating System, and Version – for compatibility, performance, and troubleshooting.
  • IP Address – for approximate location if you don’t provide it, and for security (e.g., fraud, abuse prevention).

We do not collect contacts, photos, precise GPS, or payment info unless clearly presented and consented to within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized diet charts and recommendations.
  • To suggest food/fruit items available near your location.
  • To store your diet history and preferences so you can access them later.
  • To improve functionality, ensure compatibility across devices, and fix issues.
  • To protect our services, users, and our rights (e.g., prevent fraud/abuse).
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

4. Legal Bases (GDPR/EU/UK)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for processing health-related inputs and location where required.
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) to provide the App and personalized diet functionality you request.
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) for app security, troubleshooting, and service improvement—balanced against your rights.
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) where we must comply with applicable laws.

Note: Certain health-related data may be considered “special category data.” Where applicable, we rely on your explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a)). You may withdraw consent at any time (see Your Rights & Choices).

5. Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • Service Providers: We may share limited data (e.g., device/technical info, anonymized analytics) with trusted providers who help us operate the App under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal & Safety: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property.
  • Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may transfer under the same protections.

6. Data Retention

  • Diet data and preferences are kept while your account/app usage remains active. If you uninstall, we retain saved diet plans so you can restore them after reinstall—unless you request deletion.
  • Technical logs (e.g., device info, IP) are kept only as long as needed for security and performance.
  • We delete or anonymize data when no longer needed for the purposes described.

7. Security

  • We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest where applicable.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel with a need to know.
  • No security method is 100%—we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continually improve our protections.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. Where required, we implement lawful transfer mechanisms (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) and additional safeguards.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access your data and receive a copy.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the basis.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing (where applicable).
  • Data portability (receive data in a commonly used format).

To exercise these rights, contact us at [support@email.example]. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

CCPA/CPRA (California)

California residents have rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of certain sharing. We do not “sell” personal data as defined by CPRA, nor do we share for cross-context behavioral advertising.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in certain regions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us to delete it.

11. Cookies & Analytics

The mobile App itself does not use browser cookies. If we use analytics or crash reporting SDKs, we limit data to technical metrics necessary to operate and improve the App. Details will be provided in-app if applicable.
